Is your Claim Defensible?

Deterministic verification

VaaS does not ask whether a claim “sounds plausible”. It maps the claim to a precise verification route, retrieves relevant evidence Units, and applies explicit checks such as exact values, ranges, existence checks, baseline consistency, or policy rules. The result is a repeatable defensibility signal with provenance.

Document-based pilot

For a full sustainability report, VaaS extracts report claims, groups them by verification route, and returns a defensibility ledger showing which claims are trusted, emerging, contested, or non-verifiable. This page demos the single-claim entry point; the document workflow is the natural pilot step.
