V.a.a.S

Verification as a Service is a deterministic verification system to scale sustainability report audits. It automatically checks every claim against authoritative sources, so your team can spend its time only on the claims that don't hold up.

Deterministic verification means the claim is checked against explicit, authoritative sources and fixed rules — not inferred or guessed. The same input always produces the same result, with traceable evidence, unlike probabilistic AI answers, which can vary between runs and offer no fixed evidentiary trail.

In practice, this means every claim is matched against authoritative sources — including datasets and registries such as EDGAR, WMO, NASA and other relevant scientific or regulatory bodies, as well as regulation and their subsequent versions (e.g. EU directives, delegated acts, and the standards derived from them) — rather than relying on a model's general knowledge.
